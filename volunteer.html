Volunteer Sign Up

Can NRIs volunteer?

Yes. NRI volunteers can support remotely or coordinate service days during AP visits by calling the trust office in advance.

Do I need special skills?

No. Medical, teaching, design, language, coordination, and documentation skills are useful, but structured roles can fit many volunteers.

What is the time commitment?

Options can range from one event to recurring monthly support, depending on current program needs.

Can't volunteer right now?

A donation still helps field teams reach children, patients, and families.
